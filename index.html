File Locker

Secure your files with AES-256 encryption. Everything happens entirely in your browser—no data is ever sent to a server.

How to use:
To Lock: Select any file, set a strong password, and click Lock. A secure .locked file will be downloaded.
To Unlock: Switch to Unlock mode, upload your .locked file, enter your password, and click Unlock to restore the original file.
